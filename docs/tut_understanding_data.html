Understanding data

The most basic concept of data science is understanding the data you are given. Everything in data science revolves around the data.

Generally speaking, you should be able to answer the following questions before diving to analyzing the dataset

  1. What was the primary reason for collecting this dataset?
  2. If its a tabular dataset, what are the rows and columns?
  3. The most important question - What all can I extract from this dataset?

The answers to the first two questions can be normally found in the documentation or from the person who gives you the dataset. What you really need to master is What is all the information hidden in this dataset that I can extract
